Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need to bring anything?
Nothing at all — all materials, tools, and aprons are provided. Just wear something you don't mind getting paint on.
What is your cancellation policy?
Full refunds up to 48 hours before your class. Within 48 hours, you can reschedule to another date at no charge.
Can I book as a gift?
Absolutely! Add a note in "Special Requests" and we'll email you a printable gift card to present to the recipient.
Are private events available?
Yes! Select "Private Event / Party" above and include your event details in the notes. We'll follow up to customise.

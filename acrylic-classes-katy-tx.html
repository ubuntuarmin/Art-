Class overview

Our acrylic painting classes in Katy, TX are designed for students who want practical skills and strong creative results. Each class starts with visual planning, value studies, and color mapping so students understand why a painting works before they move into heavy-body acrylic techniques. Because acrylic dries quickly, students can learn layering, glazing, and texture-building in one session while receiving direct instructor feedback. Whether you are a beginner from Cinco Ranch, a teen building a portfolio, or an adult returning to art after years away, this class gives you a clear process you can repeat at home and in the studio.

What you'll learn

  • Color mixing systems for warm/cool balance, neutrals, and skin-tone control
  • Brush handling for edges, blending, texture, and expressive mark-making
  • Layering and drying workflow to prevent muddy color and overworking
  • Composition fundamentals including focal points, value contrast, and visual flow
  • Finishing techniques that protect acrylic paintings and improve presentation

How the class works

Skill Warm-Up

Students begin with quick drills in value, brush pressure, and color matching to prepare for the main project.

Project Build

You block in forms, refine structure, and add layered color with live guidance throughout each stage.

Refine & Finish

Final sessions focus on edges, highlights, surface texture, and critique so artwork looks polished and intentional.

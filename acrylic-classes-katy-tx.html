Class Overview

Our acrylic painting classes in Katy, TX are designed for beginners, teens, and adults who want better painting results with a clear process. Acrylic is fast-drying, which makes it ideal for learning layering, color control, and texture in real time. Students practice color planning, edge control, and brush rhythm while completing guided projects that reinforce strong visual fundamentals. The class balances technical structure with creative freedom so every student can improve without feeling overwhelmed.

What You Will Learn

  • Color-mixing systems for cleaner and more intentional palettes
  • Brush handling for smooth blends, texture, and edge variation
  • Layering strategies that prevent muddy color and flattening
  • Composition principles for focal points, flow, and balance
  • Finishing techniques for polished, display-ready artwork

How This Class Works

Warm-Up Drills

Begin each session with value, brush, and color drills that prepare you for the main project.

Guided Painting Build

Develop your piece step by step with instructor checkpoints and live corrections.

Final Refinement

Refine contrast, highlights, and detail for a stronger final composition.

Class Overview

Our crafting classes in Katy, TX are perfect for students who enjoy tactile projects and creative experimentation with clear support. You learn how to combine materials, build texture, and structure projects so they look polished and hold up over time. Lessons include composition planning, assembly methods, and finishing details that elevate handmade work beyond basic DIY outcomes. This class is a favorite for weekend creativity, personal projects, and meaningful handmade gifts.

  • Color and layout planning for balanced visual design
  • Finishing details for gift-ready and display-ready projects

Class Overview

Our Dot Mandala classes in Katy, TX are designed for students who want both creative expression and focused calm. You learn tool control, spacing, pattern planning, and color sequencing so your designs remain balanced and clean. The class is beginner-friendly while still offering depth for returning artists who want more complex pattern flow. Students often choose this workshop for stress relief, mindful creativity, and beautiful finished pieces suitable for display or gifting.

What You Will Learn

  • Dot-tool and brush techniques for consistent mark quality
  • Symmetry mapping and spacing for clean radial layouts
  • Color layering methods for depth and visual rhythm
  • Pattern sequencing for flow and complexity
  • Finishing and sealing basics for long-lasting artwork

How This Class Works

Layout Mapping

Start with a central plan and radial guides to maintain symmetry throughout the design.

Pattern Build

Layer dots in sequences to create rhythm, contrast, and controlled expansion.

Polish & Finish

Refine spacing and highlight zones, then prepare the work for final presentation.

Training Painting & Drawing

Oil Painting & Drawing Classes

Discover the timeless beauty of traditional Art at Beyond the Canvas Art Studio & Gallery. We offer professional Oil painting and drawing classes designed for all levels — from beginners to advanced Artists.

Learn essential techniques, develop your style, and bring your imagination to life through line, light, and color. Whether you're exploring Art for the first time or refining your skills, our guided classes provide a creative space to grow, express, and create.

All ages welcome. Join us and paint your own masterpiece.

Resin Art & Craft Workshops

Create Stunning Resin Art

Dive into the fluid, mesmerizing world of resin art at Beyond the Canvas Art Studio. Using epoxy resin, alcohol inks, pigments, mica powders, and gold leaf, you'll create pieces that are truly one of a kind.

Our resin workshops are suitable for all skill levels. No prior experience is needed — Farnaz will guide you through every step, from safety and mixing to achieving beautiful cells, ocean waves, and geode effects.

Classes include:

  • Resin Art Workshop — fluid art, pour techniques (All Levels)
  • Ocean Resin & Geode Art — advanced layering (Intermediate)
  • Resin Jewellery & Small Objects — moulds and casting (Beginner)

All safety equipment, resin, pigments, and materials are included. Take your masterpiece home the same day!

Meet the Artist

Meet Farnaz Amin

Born in Los Angeles to an Iranian family, Farnaz Amin has been immersed in Art since childhood. Trained in multiple countries and a graduate of Art school, she has exhibited her work internationally and won several awards.

With over 25 years of teaching experience, Farnaz brings passion, creativity, and cultural depth to every class and canvas.

"For her, Art isn't just a skill — it's a way of life."

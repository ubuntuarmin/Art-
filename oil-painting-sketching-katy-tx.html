Class Overview

Our oil painting and sketching classes in Katy, TX combine technical drawing practice with professional oil painting workflow. Students begin with observational sketching and proportion, then move into composition planning and color studies before painting on canvas. This process helps artists avoid common mistakes and build stronger finished work. Whether you are preparing a portfolio, returning to art after a break, or beginning your first serious class, the curriculum is designed to create consistent, measurable progress.

What You Will Learn

  • Sketching fundamentals for proportion, form, and value
  • Color mixing and temperature control for realistic oil painting
  • Layering, glazing, and brushwork for depth and clarity
  • Composition and focal-point planning for stronger storytelling
  • Critique and refinement methods used by professional artists

How This Class Works

Foundation Drawing

Practice structural drawing and tonal mapping before painting so your composition is accurate and balanced.

Color Study & Block-In

Plan palette and major shapes to establish visual hierarchy and avoid overworking.

Layer & Refine

Build depth through controlled oil layers, then refine edges, textures, and highlights.

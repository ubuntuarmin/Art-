Class Overview

Our resin art classes in Katy, TX teach both creative design and technical control so students can produce reliable, professional-looking results. You learn proper ratios, workspace setup, and curing considerations before moving into color effects such as cells, lacing, and layered pours. Each session emphasizes clean technique and finishing quality so your final pieces are durable and display-ready. This class is popular for personal projects, modern home décor, and custom gift creation.

What You Will Learn

  • Resin safety, ventilation awareness, and workspace setup
  • Accurate measuring and mixing for consistent curing
  • Pigment blending, lacing, and cell-building effects
  • Controlled layering for geode, ocean, and abstract styles
  • Edge finishing and top-coating for professional presentation

How This Class Works

Safety & Planning

Review setup, PPE, and project design before mixing to avoid common resin errors.

Pour Execution

Cure & Finish

Complete curing checks, cleanup, and finishing details for gallery-quality results.

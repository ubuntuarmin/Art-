Class Overview

Our Summer Art Camp in Katy, TX is designed for children who love to create and explore new materials in a supportive environment. Campers rotate through painting, drawing, and mixed-media projects that build fine-motor skills, confidence, and creative thinking. Each day includes guided instruction plus room for personal expression so students feel successful while still learning artistic fundamentals. Families choose this camp as a high-value summer enrichment option that combines fun with meaningful skill development.

What You Will Learn

  • Painting and drawing fundamentals adapted for young learners
  • Color, shape, and composition concepts through hands-on projects
  • Creative problem-solving and decision-making during project work
  • Safe tool handling and studio habits for independent confidence
  • Presentation confidence through completed take-home artwork

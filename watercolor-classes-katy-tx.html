Class Overview

Our watercolor classes in Katy, TX help students solve the most common watercolor challenge: controlling water, pigment, and timing. Lessons begin with value and wash planning before moving into layered projects that build confidence and consistency. Students learn when to glaze, lift, or leave passages untouched so paintings stay luminous rather than muddy. With structured coaching and repeated exercises, both beginners and returning artists develop a clearer process and stronger results.

What You Will Learn

  • Wet-on-wet and wet-on-dry methods for controlled edges
  • Transparent layering and glazing for depth and light
  • Value planning and negative painting for stronger shape control
  • Brush handling for details, transitions, and focal points
  • Finishing strategies that keep watercolor fresh and balanced

How This Class Works

Palette & Paper Prep

Set up your paper and values so each painting has a strong tonal plan from the start.

Layer Progression

Build color in stages while learning timing decisions that preserve clarity and light.

Detail & Balance

Complete final edges and accents with critique support to improve overall composition.
